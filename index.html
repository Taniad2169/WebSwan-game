How To Play 🦢
1. Pick a Category
Tap any chip at the top — Digital World, Travel, Phone Apps, Marketing, or WebSwan.

2. Read the Question
The survey question appears. The top answers are hidden on the board (numbered tiles).

3. Type Your Answer!
Guest types their answer in the input box and hits Submit or Enter. If it matches any answer on the board — that tile flips open automatically! 🎉

4. Use the Buttons
• 💡 Reveal — show the next hidden answer (host only)
• ✕ Strike — wrong guess, 3 strikes = other team steals!
• ⇄ Team — manually pass turn to other team
• Next ▶ — move to the next question
• ↺ — reset all scores

5. Win the Bank!
Reveal all answers before 3 strikes to claim the bank points for your team. 🏆

Tip: The highlighted team is currently playing. Switch teams after strikes!
